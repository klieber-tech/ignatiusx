HOW IT WORKS

LOG YOUR IDEAS FOR REFERENCE AND CURATION

Capture ideas on the fly. Then circle back to them and organize them in our tree structure, later. Ideas are considered Building Blocks. These can then be grouped as Edifices, which represent a specific research output -- a paper or presentation. Edifices are maintained within a Cityscape, a portfolio of one's interests, research and outputs.

UNEARTH THE SYNERGIES IN YOUR OWN WORK AND OTHERS' WORK

Discover the power of our advanced search and filtering systems, leveraging the capabilities of XML and XSLT. Use keywords, dates, and other metadata to group specific ideas, as well as visualize synergies and relationships between various concepts, thus enhancing the research process.

SEARCH AVAILABLE LITERATURE WITH OUR ARXIV API

Our literature-checking capabilities include a live API connecting directly to arXiv, the digital open-access source for scholarly articles. arXiv.org now hosts more than two million scholarly articles in eight subject areas (source: ArXiv). Scan academic literature on your keywords of choice, ensuring that your research is both novel and comprehensive.
